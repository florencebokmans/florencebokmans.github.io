How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ? How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?
Go to http://print.hz.nl and login with your HZ account. You can upload your file here and print at any printer in HZ by using your student card. Go to a printer at HZ and use you student card to activate. Choose scan document, the scanned documents will be send to your hz email.
How can I buy something on the HZ web shop? Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?
Go to MyHZ then go to web shop. Once you're there you can the available thing to your shopping cart an purchase them. Go to MyHZ > actualiteiten > coronavirus > positief getest. Here you can find what to do in case you are sick.
How can you book a project space in one of the wings? What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
Go to the home page of Self Service Portal. Then go to Reservations & Visitors here you will be able to book a project space. You have to active your HZ student card. After that you will be able to scan it to park your car.
